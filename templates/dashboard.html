Project Overview

KidneyAI is a multimodal healthcare decision support system designed to assist in kidney disease assessment using clinical biomarkers and renal ultrasound imaging. The framework integrates machine learning and deep learning models into a unified platform capable of generating automated diagnostic reports and supporting early disease detection.
